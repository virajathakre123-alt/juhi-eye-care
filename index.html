What patients experience under my care ?

When you visit, you can expect careful check-ups, clear explanations, and enough time to ask questions. Every step of your treatment is explained in a simple way, so you understand what is happening and why.

The focus is always on your comfort, safety, and long-term eye health. Treatments are suggested only when needed, and decisions are made together, helping you feel confident and at ease throughout your care.

Conditions we treat

Cataract Surgery

Cataract is a condition where the natural lens of the eye becomes cloudy, making vision blurred or dull. It usually happens with age and can slowly affect daily activities like reading, driving, watching TV, or daily work.

Procedure:

Painless surgery to replace the cloudy lens with a clear artificial lens.

Cornea Care

Conditions affecting the clear front surface of the eye, including infections, dryness, and corneal scarring, are assessed with gentle, focused care.

Procedure:

Detailed examination and treatment planning for comfort and clarity.

Dry Eye Treatment

Care for burning, watering, irritation, and discomfort caused by poor tear quality or reduced tear production.

Procedure:

Eye drops, lid care, and stepwise relief-based treatment.

Refractive Surgery

Treatment options for glasses and contact lens freedom, with careful evaluation before any procedure is suggested.

Procedure:

Vision correction planning after detailed measurements and counselling.

PDEK

Corneal transplant care for selected patients with damaged corneal layers and reduced vision.

Procedure:

Specialized transplant evaluation and surgical planning.

Medical Retina Care

Retina evaluation for diabetic eye changes, swelling, bleeding, and other conditions that need timely medical attention.

Procedure:

Detailed retinal examination with treatment follow-up.

Anti-VEGF Treatment

Injection-based treatment used for selected retinal conditions that require close monitoring and timely care.

Procedure:

In-office treatment with careful follow-up planning.

Laser Treatment

Laser procedures for selected retina conditions and other eye care needs that benefit from precise treatment.

Procedure:

Focused treatment with patient guidance and recovery advice.
